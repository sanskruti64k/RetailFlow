# **Retail_Management_System**

---

## Problem Defintion

Retail businesses struggle with tracking sales, managing inventory, understanding customer behavior, and making data-driven decisions. Without a centralized database system, they face challenges in:

1. Manual inventory tracking leading to stockouts or overstocking
2. Difficulty in analyzing sales patterns
3. Inefficient customer management
4. Lack of insights for business decisions

---

## Problem Statement 

Design and implement a Retail Management Database System that efficiently manages products, customers, orders, payments, and inventory to provide actionable business insights through data analysis.
